Career Plan

Helping you choose the best medical and health career based on KUCCPS requirements

At Nurselabs, we guide students in selecting suitable medical and health-related courses by analyzing KCSE performance, KUCCPS subject requirements, personal interests, and long-term career goals.

General KUCCPS Subject Requirements
  • Biology (Mandatory)
  • Chemistry (Mandatory)
  • Mathematics or Physics
  • English or Kiswahili
Final placement depends on KUCCPS cut-off points and available slots.

Medical & Health Career Pathways

Medicine & Surgery

Best for students with strong science performance who are prepared for long-term training and specialization.

Nursing

Ideal for compassionate individuals interested in patient care, with flexible academic progression.

Clinical Medicine

Suitable for students who prefer practical medical training with a shorter study duration than medicine.

Pharmacy

Best for learners strong in chemistry and interested in drugs, patient safety, and pharmaceutical care.

Medical Laboratory Sciences

Ideal for analytical students interested in diagnostics, laboratory work, and medical research.

Public Health

Focuses on disease prevention, community health promotion, and public health policy.

Nurselabs helps you identify alternative pathways if direct entry requirements are not met and supports you through every step of your healthcare career journey.
