Emergency Room Nurse Career Guide: How to Become an ER Nurse

Becoming an Emergency Room Nurse in Kenya is a rewarding career path with dynamic clinical experiences, strong job demand, and the chance to make a lifesaving impact. ER nurses work on the frontlines of acute care – one shift they might stabilize a car accident victim, and the next they could be managing a patient’s heart attack. It’s a fast-paced role where nurses triage arriving patients, perform urgent interventions, and collaborate with doctors and first responders to treat everything from broken bones to life-threatening injuries.
